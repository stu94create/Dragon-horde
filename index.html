    About the voices▾

    Apple's deeper voices like Rocko and Rocco exist on iOS but Apple does not expose them to web apps — they only work in Siri, VoiceOver, and Spoken Content. From this app the best dragon-sounding choices are:

    1. Bad News — slow, ominous, default pick.
    2. Cellos — sings the lines in deep cello tones.
    3. Trinoids / Zarvox — robotic, otherworldly.
    4. Daniel — deep British male, the most "normal" fallback.

    Use the Choose voice dropdown above to override the default pick.
